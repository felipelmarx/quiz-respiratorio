## Current Project: Quiz Respiratório

A digital quiz that evaluates respiratory dysfunction and its relationship to anxiety. The quiz collects leads and provides personalized results with scores across 4 dimensions:

1. **Padrão Respiratório** (Breathing Pattern) — 0-12 points
2. **Sintomas Relacionados** (Related Symptoms) — 0-12 points
3. **Consciência Respiratória** (Breathing Awareness) — 0-3 points
4. **Tolerância ao CO₂** (CO₂ Tolerance) — 0-6 points
5. **Total** — 0-33 points
